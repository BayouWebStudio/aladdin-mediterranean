Our Heritage

A gem for true Mediterranean gastronomy enthusiasts

Aladdin Mediterranean Grill brings the authentic flavors of the Mediterranean to the heart of Houston's Oak Forest neighborhood.

Located in a newly opened strip mall setting near the Oak Forest neighborhood, our restaurant serves a good variety of Mediterranean food with generous portions and authentic flavors. Our menu features many variety of food items including lamb, beef, and fish along with traditional Mediterranean side dishes.

We pride ourselves on using fresh ingredients, traditional spices, and time-honored cooking techniques. Whether you're enjoying our signature grilled chicken kebabs, savoring our lunch specials, or indulging in our fresh Mediterranean salads, every dish is prepared with care and authenticity.

Our commitment to quality and flavor has made us a celebrated choice for Mediterranean cuisine in Houston, as featured in local press coverage highlighting our dedication to Mediterranean culinary excellence.
